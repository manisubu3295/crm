VLSI & FPGA Design
Verilog and VHDL on Xilinx Vivado — from combinational logic through synthesis, FPGA implementation, and timing closure on real silicon.
⏱ 6 Weeks ● Intermediate ▸ ECE / EEE
Module Breakdown
  • 01
    Digital Logic Review
    Combinational and sequential circuits, Karnaugh maps, FSM design, timing diagrams
  • 02
    Verilog HDL Programming
    Data types, always/initial blocks, assign statements, module hierarchy, testbench writing
  • 03
    VHDL Fundamentals
    Entity/architecture model, concurrent vs. sequential statements, signal vs. variable semantics
  • 04
    Simulation & Verification
    ModelSim waveform analysis, self-checking testbenches, functional coverage, assertions
  • 05
    Synthesis on Xilinx Vivado
    RTL-to-netlist flow, timing constraints (XDC), I/O planning, critical path optimisation
  • 06
    FPGA Prototyping & Low-Power Design
    Bitstream generation, ILA/ChipScope debugging, clock gating, power domain planning (UPF)
